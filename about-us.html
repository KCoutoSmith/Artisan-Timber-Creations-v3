About Us

Artisan Timber Creations is a locally-owned business in Pickering, Ontario that supports our community.

All of our products are made from locally-sourced material and built right in Pickering.

Our team is made up of a small group of designers, carpenters and salespeople who are always making it their top priority to make the customer satisfied. We are proud to demonstrate this to you by:

  • Meeting with you to help create a vision for your product
  • Reviewing design blueprints with you to review ideas of how the finished product will look like
  • Arranging an in-home visit to confirm sizing, colour and style ideas to fit with your current layout
  • Keeping you informed of progress along the way, and involved in decisions
  • Providing top-quality finished products in record time

Feel free to browse our selection of products on-line or in-store, or discuss your ideas further with the design team so they can make a custom order that's just right for your needs.
