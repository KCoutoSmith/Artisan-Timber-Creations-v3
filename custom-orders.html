Custom Orders

Our design team would be more than happy to meet with you to help create that custom piece that fits your needs perfectly.

To help us serve you better, please fill out the form below with the following information, and we will arrange an in-person meeting to discuss further.

Please include the following information in your request, in the following order:

  1. Your name
  2. Your email address
  3. What type of furniture you would like to have made
  4. What is the general style you are looking for? (modern, vintage, or other)
  5. What approximate size would you like the piece to be?
  6. What is your top priority in making this piece? (style, price, approximate completion date)
  7. What is your postal code
We'll never share your email with anyone else.
